01 · The Problem
What Every Indian Farmer Faces Daily

Agriculture employs 58% of India's workforce, yet farmers lose an estimated ₹50,000 crore per year to preventable crop diseases and poor decision-making — caused by the lack of timely, expert agricultural guidance.

30-40%Yield lost to disease annually
₹15,000Avg. farmer loss per acre/season
1 ExpertPer 1000+ farmers in rural India
72 hrsAvg. delay before treatment starts

🦠 Disease Blindness

Farmers cannot identify which of 38+ diseases attacks their crops. By the time a disease is visually obvious, 60% of damage has occurred.

🌦️ No Weather Integration

Farmers spray and irrigate without knowing incoming rain or humidity spikes that trigger outbreaks.

🌿 Wrong Crop Choices

Without soil and climate data, farmers grow unsuited crops — resulting in sub-50% yields and massive input waste.

💊 Fertilizer Misuse

Over-application costs ₹3,000–8,000/acre unnecessarily and degrades soil for future seasons.

🗣️ Language Barrier

85% of Indian farmers are non-English speakers. Existing agricultural apps exclude them entirely.

🏥 Expert Access Gap

Reaching a KVK expert takes 1–5 days. By then, blight has spread across an entire field.

Key Insight

ICRISAT: early detection (within 24–48 hrs) reduces crop loss by 78% and cuts treatment cost by 60%. Technology is the only way to close this gap at scale.

02 · Our Solution
ArogyaKrishi — The Precision Farm Doctor

ArogyaKrishi puts the equivalent of a professional agronomist, weather forecaster, soil scientist, and disease expert in every farmer's pocket — in their own language, 24/7, on a ₹5,000 Android phone.

Backend Services
  • disease_service.py — ResNet-50 inference + Groq fertilizer AI
  • weather_service.py — OpenWeatherMap live data
  • voice_service.py — Multilingual AI + offline knowledge engine
  • gemini_service.py — Groq primary + Gemini fallback LLM
  • api_spa.py — Full REST API gateway (10 endpoints)
Frontend Architecture
  • Vite + React 18 — SPA with React Router v6
  • Zustand store — Global state (profile, scan results)
  • TypeScript service layer — Typed API clients per domain
  • TailwindCSS + Material3 — Mobile-first design system
  • PWA-ready — Installable, works offline with cached data
AI Resilience — Zero Single Point of Failure

Groq LLaMA-3.3 70B (primary) → Google Gemini 1.5 Flash (fallback) → 50+ rule-based offline agricultural knowledge engine. The app never stops working, even without internet.
